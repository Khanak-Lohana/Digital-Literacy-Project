## Prevention Measures for UPI Fraud

To stay safe while using UPI and digital payment apps, follow these important precautions:

Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank or customer support.
Remember: Scanning a QR code means you are paying money, not receiving it.
Do not approve unknown payment requests sent by strangers.
Avoid clicking suspicious links received through SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
Verify customer care numbers only from official bank or app websites.
Do not install screen-sharing or remote access apps suggested by unknown persons.
Check transaction details carefully before entering your UPI PIN.
Use strong phone security, such as screen lock, fingerprint, or face unlock.
Enable transaction alerts and notifications to monitor account activity.
Report fraud immediately by calling the Cybercrime Helpline 1930 or reporting on the cybercrime portal.
